INTRODUCTION

What is 24 Festival Drum?

24 Festival Drums is write 24 seasons on the 24 single percussions by using calligraphy. Long time ago, China is a agricultural society, our ancestors observed astrology from their farming and what they felt to the nature. Every 15 days to find out a seasonal, 2 seasons in a month, a total of 24 seasons in a year. Therefore, 24 seasons had compiled into 24 Festival Drums. Everytime we drumming, it feels like a traditional seasons calling us from heart and the pulse.

Our ancestors had inherited a very colourful traditional culture in our life. Drumming is a festival of sound, is a traditional drum pulse. As we drumming, it also like we continue to inherite the precious culture to the next generation as well as we never forget the cultural. Nowadays, you can easily watch the performance of 24 festival drum in Asia as their important bonquet or in many large event.


Malaysian Cultural Heritage

24 Festival Drum is a very magnificent and inspiring drum in the world. It becomes the favourite of Malaysian no matter races and age.



Introduction of development

Nowadays, there are about 200 Malaysian branch twenty-four Festive Drum team, over 20,000 drummers in Malaysia. Drummers are mostly young people. In 2009, the Malaysian Ministry of Culture announced 24 Festival Drums becomes National Intangible Cultural Heritage. 24 Festival Drums has become one of only two nonmaterial cultural heritage of ethnic Chinese in Malaysia by the creation of the country.
