History

History of 24 Festive Drums

The concept of the 24-Festival Drums, was created by two talented Malaysians; Mr Tan Chai Puan, a cultural activist, businessman, poet and cartoonist, and the late Mr Tan Hooi Song (passed away in August 2008), a renown musician who was bestowed the Warisan Orang Hidup award, by the ministry for his role in creating the 24-Festival Drums. They shared the same vision and, with missionary zeal, worked together to create the 24-Festival Drums in 1988.

Concept Origin

The concept originated from the Ninth National Chinese Dance Festival, Mr. Tan Chai Puan and the late Mr. Tan Hooi Song were responsible for planning the opening ceremony of this grand event, "Ninth Dance". To respond to the theme, Mr. Tan Chai Puan decided to plan the performance in the form of nine drums, and the music was composed by Mr. Tan Hooi Song.The result of the performance was very wonderful and loved by many people.

After that, Mr. Tan Chai Puan discovered 24 festivals in the lunar calendar, and he thought it was a great knowledge from ancestor and it was romantic! So he tried to combine the 24 Festive(24 solar terms) with lion drums. The third element added into 24 Festive Drums was Chinese Calligraphy. In April 1988, Malaysia Chinese Association joint the five main Association in Johor Bahru funded the first twenty-four Festival Drums support team was established.
