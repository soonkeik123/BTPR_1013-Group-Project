About Us

History of SUC 24 FD
We were founded on June 6, 1996. It has been 24 years now. The drum team was originally established as the 24 Festive Drums Institute of Foon Yew High school, but now we have grown up with time and become independent SUC 24 Festive Drums Team!
Our Aim
Our biggest goal is to inherit the culture and let more people know about the 24 Festive Drums. Besides that, the biggest similarity between 24-season drum and Southern University College is that they both adheres to the traditional Chinese culture and is committed to inheritance and development. Both of them were hoping that the younger generation of Chinese can appreciate the cultures and learn the importance of cherishing the fundamental culture.

It's not just about artistic performance, but the drum team doctrine teaches teamwork and corporation. Encourage drummers to put in time and energy to rehearse, run in and cultivate tacit understanding, mutual tolerance among the members is also indispensable.
More Information
For large activities in the school, such as the opening ceremony, our team will participate in the performance, because this is our responsibility and our gratitude to the school. Our team has also been invited by organizations outside the school to perform 24 Festive Drums for many times, such as the Mid Autumn Festival party, which also adds another kind of impact and interest to our drums team.

We will continue to work hard to bring 24 festive drums to more people, so that everyone can experience the shock of drum and the joy of the festival
 
Tee Liang Chao
