Basic Information of 24 Festive Drums
There is some basic knowledge about 24 Festive Drums that you have to know about:
  • 24 Festive Drums is created in 12th June 1988.
  • The founders of 24 Festive Drums are Mr.Tan Chai Puan and Mr.Tan Hooi Song
  • The composition of 24 Festive Drums:

    1. 24 Solar Terms written in calligraphy.
    2. Drums(Same as Lion's Dance Drums)
    3. Drum Stand
  • The aim of 24 Festive Drums:
    1. Carry forward Chinese culture.
    2. Promote the percussion technique of 24 Festive Drums.
    3. Promote the spirit of disciplinary groups.
  • The element of a 24 Festive Drums Performance
    1. The Rhythm
    2. Stylish Moves performed by a particular drummers
    3. Many Drummers
